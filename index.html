About FACT

Since the inception of blockchain technology many developers have raised start-up funds using token sales. For them it was unclear how to legally structure the whole operation. Usually they would operate from offshore or countries like Singapore.

Users are receptive to take part in token but they find it apprehensive due the nature of complex and undefined structure.

There are frameworks available such as SAFT which are applicable in few countries. We believe India needs its own legal framework "FACT" which covers diverse range of users. Thus FACT

FACT puts forth all the best tool available while respecting the policy goals and drive the law. FACT project is the effort to formalise a framework for compliance token sales.
